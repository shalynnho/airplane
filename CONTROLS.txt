Controls also in the main menu.

Launch Prep Mode
WASD - Rotating and aiming the airplane
Arrow Keys - Rotate the camera
T - Free Camera Mode
Space Bar - Enter Flight Mode
Left-Click - Place fan / Rotate Existing Fan
Right-Click - Remove fan

Free Camera Mode
Q and E - Roll the camera
W and S - Move the camera back and forth
Arrow Keys - Aim the camera
T - Launch Prep Mode

Flight Mode
R - End flight
Arrow Keys - Rotate the Camera
